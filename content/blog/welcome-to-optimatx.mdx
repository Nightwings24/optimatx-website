OptimatX is the Mathematics Society of IIT Patna. This blog is where we think out loud - expository write-ups, solutions worth remembering, and the occasional rabbit hole.

## Why "OptimatX"?

The name leans into optimization: the search for the best answer under constraints. Formally, that is an $\arg\max$ (or $\arg\min$) problem,

$$
x^\star = \arg\max_{x \in \Omega} f(x),
$$

where $\Omega$ is the set of feasible choices and $f$ is what we care about. A surprising amount of applied mathematics is some version of this one line.

## What to expect

- A **Problem of the Week**, with an answer checker and a growing archive.
- Member write-ups on analysis, algebra, probability, and optimization.
- Reading circles and competition prep - SRMC, Simon Marais, the Integration Bee.
