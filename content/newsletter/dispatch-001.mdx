## One thing worth thinking about

Why does the Basel problem's answer, $\sum_{n \ge 1} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}$,
have a $\pi$ in it at all? There's a proof that literally finds a circle. We'll
run it as a blog post soon - or beat us to it and
[write it up yourself](/join).
